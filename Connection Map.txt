SD Card -> Uno:
---------------
VCC -> 3.3V
GND -> GND
SCK -> 13
DO (MISO) -> 12
DI (MOSI) -> 11
CS -> 4

--------------------------------

Speaker -> Uno:
---------------
Red -> 9
Black -> GND

--------------------------------

Pads -> Uno:
------------
StartPad -> A0
Pad1 -> A1
Pad2 -> A2
Pad3 -> A3
Pad4 -> A4
